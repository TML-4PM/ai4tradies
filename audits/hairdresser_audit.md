# AI4Tradies Audit Report — Hairdresser

## Business Snapshot
Trade: Hairdresser / Hair Salon

---

## Your Top 3 Profit Leaks

### 1. No-Shows and Last-Minute Cancellations
Estimated Loss: $8K - $18K/year
Every empty chair is money walking out the door. Most salons lose 15-20% of bookings to no-shows.
Fix: Automated booking confirmations and reminders sent 48hr and 2hr before appointment.

---

### 2. Clients Not Rebooking
Estimated Loss: $10K - $25K/year
Most clients wait until they "need" a cut. If you're not prompting them, you're losing the rebook.
Fix: Automated rebook reminder sent 5-6 weeks after last visit.

---

### 3. Missed DMs and Booking Requests
Estimated Loss: $5K - $12K/year
People message on Instagram or Facebook but don't get a reply for hours. They book somewhere else.
Fix: Instant auto-reply system that captures booking requests 24/7.
