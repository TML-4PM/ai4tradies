## Your Top 3 Profit Leaks

### 1. Missed Calls and Slow Responses
Estimated Loss: $10K - $25K/year
Electricians get called for urgent jobs. If you don't answer or reply fast, they call the next sparky on the list.
Fix: Instant auto-reply system that captures the lead and books a callback.

---

### 2. No Follow-Up on Quoted Jobs
Estimated Loss: $8K - $20K/year
Most electricians quote then forget. 80% of jobs go to the tradie who follows up.
Fix: Automated follow-up sequence at 24hr, 48hr, and 5 days after quote.

---

### 3. Invoices Sent Late or Not at All
Estimated Loss: $5K - $15K/year
Chasing payments manually or forgetting to invoice same-day kills cash flow.
Fix: Auto-invoice on job completion with payment link and reminders.
